About

This is web-based platform designed to help hostel administrators manage various aspects of their hostel operations. The system enables hostel administrators to automate processes such as room allocation, attendance, leave approval, check-in and check-out, notices and maintenance requests.

Features
  • Room management: Allows hostel administrators to manage room availability, maintenance.
  • Student management: The website allows administrators to manage student profiles, leave approvals, attendance.
  • Maintenance management: The website allows guests to submit maintenance requests, and administrators should be able to manage and track these requests.
  • Online leave application and approval: The website allows students to apply for leave online and wardens to approve them.
  • Virtual Notice Board: The website allows administrators and wardens to publish important notices.
